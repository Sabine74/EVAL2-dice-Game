ROLL & HOLD

The rules


The first player to hold 100 points in his global score wins!

To do so, roll the dice. You can roll it as many times as you want. The result adds up in your current score.
However, if you roll the dice and obtain 1, your current score is lost, and it's the end of your turn.
If you are happy with your current score, you can hold it by clicking the 'Hold' button, to add it to your global score, and it's the end of your turn.


Good luck and have fun !!!
